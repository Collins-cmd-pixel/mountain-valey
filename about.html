About Our Resort

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Mountain Valley Resort was born from a vision to create a sanctuary where guests could escape the hustle and bustle of daily life and reconnect with nature.

Located in the serene landscapes of Kimunye, Kirinyaga, our resort offers breathtaking views of Mount Kenya and the surrounding valleys. We pride ourselves on providing exceptional hospitality while preserving the natural beauty that makes this location so special.

Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to ensure every guest experiences the perfect blend of luxury accommodation, authentic cuisine, and unforgettable memories.
